Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?
We offer easy 7-day returns on all unstitched items. Items must be in original condition with tags intact.

Currently, we only ship within Pakistan. Stay tuned for international shipping updates!
Can I get custom stitching?
We do not offer stitching services at the moment. All items are unstitched and ready for your personal tailoring.
